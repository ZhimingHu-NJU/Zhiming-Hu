MISSION

From materials to intelligent systems

Construct

Discover morphing and sensing mechanisms in soft materials by tuning molecular alignment, geometry, and multi-physics coupling across scales.

Examples: light-driven LCE oscillators, helical fiber actuators, degradable micro-channels.

Compute

Develop design rules and data-driven tools to program shape, color and signal transduction directly into materials and structures.

Examples: inverse design of LCE patterns, optical readout calibration, image-based colorimetry.

Contextualize

Embed materials-level intelligence into real environments for sustainable monitoring and soft robotics, with attention to manufacturability and deployment.

Examples: field-deployable sensor fliers, water-quality monitors, UAV-compatible modules.
