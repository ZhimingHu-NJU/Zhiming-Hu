SASM LAB · SMART ACTUATION & SENSING MATERIALS

Intelligent soft materials for actuation, photonics, and environmental sensing.

At SASM Lab, we explore how liquid crystal polymers, soft photonic structures, and bio-inspired micro-systems can encode actuation and sensing directly into materials, enabling sustainable environmental devices and soft robotic platforms.

RESEARCH

Research directions

We investigate how structure, alignment, and multi-physics coupling in soft matter give rise to programmable actuation and sensing. Our work spans materials design, mechanics, optics, and device integration.

ACTUATION · LCE
Light-driven helical actuators & oscillators
Programmable liquid crystal elastomer architectures that convert light into complex 3D motion, including autonomous oscillation and helical coiling.
Towards soft robots and intelligent materials-level engines.
PHOTONICS · SENSING
Flexible photonic & colorimetric materials
Hydrogel and polymer photonic systems that encode environmental information in color, reflection, and scattering, enabling battery-free readout.
Image-based colorimetry and optical calibration pipelines.
ENVIRONMENT · DEVICES
Bio-inspired environmental sensor platforms
Chemiluminescent micro-fliers and degradable sensor modules that can be deployed into real environments for pH and heavy-metal monitoring.
Bridging materials science, microfluidics, and field deployment.
