About SASM Lab
Smart Actuation & Sensing Materials Lab · Nanjing University

The SASM Lab focuses on flexible intelligent photonics, liquid-crystal polymer systems, and bio-inspired environmental sensors. We explore materials-level intelligence by integrating actuation, perception, and information encoding directly into soft matter.

Our long-term vision is to build scalable platforms for soft robotics, adaptive architectures, and sustainable environmental monitoring.

Research

From programmable soft actuators to self-sustained oscillators

Representative directions include:

1) Liquid-crystal elastomer based self-oscillating systems and bio-inspired helical actuators.

2) Flexible photonic materials for light-controlled actuation and multi-modal sensing.

3) Bio-degradable micro-fliers and chemiluminescent “firefly” environmental sensor platforms.
